Jellyfish

Jellyfish are not actually fish. The have neither heart nor brain. Therefor, they are better defined as plankton

  • Scientific name:Medusozoa
  • Lifespan: 6-12 months
  • Size: 1 millimeter- 2 metres
  • Diet:carnivorous

Jellyfish are the only organism on Earth to have survived without a brain for over 650 million years,even before the dinosaurs lived on the earth!
